Privacy Policy

At Rootelier, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

We will process your personal data only if we have a legal basis to do so. This includes situations where:

  • You have given consent for us to process your personal data for a specific purpose.
  • We need to perform a contract with you.
  • We need to comply with a legal obligation.
  • We need to protect your vital interests or those of another person.
  • We have a legitimate interest in processing your personal data, provided that your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests.

4. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your personal data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.
